3 Supplement Recommendations

3.1 Creatine Monohydrate - HIGHLY RECOMMENDED

Daily Dosage: 5g daily (any time of day)

Benefits:

  • Increased strength and power - Helps you lift heavier weights
  • Enhanced muscle growth - Supports muscle protein synthesis
  • Improved workout performance - Reduces fatigue during training
  • Better recovery - Faster regeneration between sets and workouts
  • Increased muscle volume - Draws water into muscle cells for fuller appearance

Safety Profile:

  • Extensively researched - Over 30 years of scientific studies
  • No harmful side effects when used as directed
  • Safe for long-term use - No need to cycle on/off
  • Suitable for all ages - Safe for healthy individuals
  • No loading phase needed - Just 5g daily is sufficient

Best Practices:

  • Mix with water, juice, or add to protein shake
  • Take consistently every day (timing doesn‚Äôt matter)
  • Stay well-hydrated when using creatine
  • Choose creatine monohydrate (most researched form)

4 Health Monitoring

4.1 GP Visit - STRONGLY RECOMMENDED

Schedule a blood work appointment with your GP before starting this program:

Why This Matters:

  • Baseline health assessment - Know your starting point
  • Identify any underlying issues - Catch problems early
  • Optimize your nutrition plan - Adjust based on your specific needs
  • Monitor progress safely - Track improvements in health markers

Key Blood Tests to Request:

  • Full blood count (CBC)
  • Comprehensive metabolic panel
  • Lipid profile (cholesterol, triglycerides)
  • Thyroid function (TSH, T3, T4)
  • Vitamin D levels
  • B12 and folate
  • Testosterone levels (for muscle building optimization)

Follow-up: Consider repeat blood work after 3-6 months to track improvements in health markers alongside your physical progress.

8 Adjustments for Small Portions

Since you prefer smaller portions, consider:

  1. Frequent smaller meals - eat every 2-3 hours

  3. Calorie-dense foods - nuts, oils, rice, dairy
  4. Gradual increases - slowly increase portion sizes weekly

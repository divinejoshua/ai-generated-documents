1.2 Credit Consumption Logic

Credits are the standardized unit of consumption across all services. This ratio determines the usage ceiling for each subscription tier.

  • Plagiarism checker: 2 credits per word
  • AI Detection / Checking: 1 credit per word
  • Humanized Prompts (Free Cap): 10 usages per month

Note: The cost structure is based on a blended usage model, reflecting API and infrastructure costs.

3.2 Required Breakeven Ratio

The crucial metric for long-term sustainability is the required paid-to-free user ratio needed to cover all free operational costs.

Required Ratio (Paid : Free Users)

1 : 12.5

One paid user offsets the cost of 12.5 free users ($10.00 profit / $0.80 free cost = 12.5).

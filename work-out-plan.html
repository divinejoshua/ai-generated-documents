6 Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I know when my progress is stalling?

A: If for a few weeks you are not adding reps or load on any exercise (with consistent sleep/nutrition), progress is stalling. Adjust: add calories, extend rest, or recheck technique.

Q: Should I train when sore?

A: If soreness is significant, rest. Mild soreness is okay. Prioritize sleep and nutrition to reduce soreness.

Q: When should I start using heavier weights?

A: When you can hit 10 clean reps on a working set at a given load, increase load next session to bring reps back toward 8, then build again.
